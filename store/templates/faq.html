Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Glow Cart. Can't find an answer? Contact us

📦 Orders

How do I place an order?+
Browse products on our homepage → Click "Add to Cart" → Go to Cart → Click "Proceed to Checkout" → Fill in your delivery details → Click "Place Order". You will receive a confirmation email immediately.
Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?+
Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, the order is processed and cannot be cancelled. Contact us at glowcart0811@gmail.com immediately if you need to cancel.
How do I track my order?+
Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking ID and link. You can also go to My Orders to see the current status and tracking information.
What payment methods do you accept?+
We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD) only. You pay when the product is delivered to your doorstep. No advance payment required.

🚚 Delivery

How long does delivery take?+
Delivery times depend on your location:

• Same city (Jodhpur): 1–2 days
• Same state (Rajasthan): 2–3 days
• Metro cities: 2–4 days
• Other states: 3–5 days
• Remote areas: 5–7 days
Is there a delivery charge?+
Delivery is FREE on orders above ₹999. For orders below ₹999, a shipping charge of ₹49 is applied at checkout.
Do you deliver all over India?+
Yes! We deliver to 24,000+ pincodes across India through our courier partner Shiprocket. Enter your pincode at checkout to confirm availability.
What if I am not available at the time of delivery?+
The courier will attempt delivery 2–3 times. If all attempts fail, the package will be returned to us. Contact us to reschedule delivery.

🔄 Returns & Refunds

How do I return a product?+
Go to My Orders → Find your delivered order → Click "Return / Exchange" → Select "Return" → Describe the reason → Submit. Our team will review within 24–48 hours.
How long does a refund take?+
Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive and verify the returned product. For COD orders, refund is sent via UPI or bank transfer.
I received a damaged product. What should I do?+
Raise a replacement request within 48 hours of delivery. Go to My Orders → Click "Return / Exchange" → Select "Replace" → Attach photos of the damage. We will ship a replacement within 2–3 business days.
Can I exchange a product for a different one?+
Yes, exchanges are available within 7 days of delivery. Go to My Orders → Click "Return / Exchange" → Select "Exchange" → Describe what you want. Our team will process it after receiving the original product.

👤 Account

Do I need an account to order?+
Yes, you need to create a free account to place orders. This helps us track your orders and send you updates. Sign up here — it takes less than 1 minute.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?+
Contact us at glowcart0811@gmail.com with your registered email and we will reset your password manually within 24 hours.
How do I use a promo code?+
At checkout, enter your promo code in the "Promo Code" field and click Apply. Available codes:

• WELCOME10 — 10% off your first order
• FLASH40 — 40% off (during flash sales)
• NEW200 — ₹200 flat off
