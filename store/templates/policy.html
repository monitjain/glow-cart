Return & Exchange Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Glow Cart

At Glow Cart, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your order, we offer easy returns, exchanges, and replacements as per the policy below.

🔄 Return Policy

  • Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery date.
  • Product must be unused, unwashed and in original packaging with all tags intact.
  • Proof of purchase (order ID) is required for all returns.
  • Items purchased during sale or with promo codes are eligible for exchange only, not refund.
  • Refund will be processed within 5–7 business days after we receive and verify the product.

🔃 Exchange Policy

  • Exchange requests must be raised within 7 days of delivery.
  • Exchange is available for different size, color, or variant of the same product.
  • Product must be in original condition with all packaging.
  • Shipping charges for exchange may apply depending on the reason.
  • Exchange will be processed within 3–5 business days after receiving the original product.

🔁 Replacement Policy (Damaged / Defective)

  • If you receive a damaged or defective product, raise a replacement request within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Attach clear photos of the damaged product while submitting the request.
  • Replacement will be shipped within 2–3 business days after verification.
  • No need to return the damaged product for replacement requests.

💰 Refund Policy

  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method or via UPI/bank transfer for COD orders.
  • COD refunds are transferred directly to your bank account within 5–7 business days.
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.
  • You will receive an email confirmation once your refund is processed.

❌ Non-Returnable Items

  • Items that have been used, washed, or damaged by the customer.
  • Products without original packaging or missing tags.
  • Items returned after 7 days of delivery.
  • Customized or personalized products.

📋 How to Raise a Request

  • Login to your account → Go to My Orders.
  • Find the delivered order → Click "Return / Exchange" button.
  • Select request type and describe the issue.
  • Our team will review and respond within 24–48 hours.
